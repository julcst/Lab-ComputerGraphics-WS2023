\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We described the approach of \textcite{belcour2018} and its extensions \parencite{weier2020,yamaguchi2019} in detail and implemented it. The statistical method used results in a real-time capable accurate way to approximate light transport in layered materials. Being able to render layered materials efficiently has a great impact on the variety of surfaces being able to reproduce.
