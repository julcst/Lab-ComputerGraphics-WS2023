\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We described and implemented the method by \textcite{deliot2023} and resulted in a fast and stable glint shader, that achieves constant performance through the use of an anisotropy compensating parametrization while correctly blending between different levels of detail and providing visually convincing results.
%
It still remains costly in comparison to smooth microfacet models but can yet be used in real-time graphics to simulate interesting material appearances.
